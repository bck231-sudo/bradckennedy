Privacy policy

How data is handled in Medication Tracker.

Data storage

Medication and wellbeing entries are stored for continuity and consult review. Storage can be local-only or synced depending on app settings.

Sharing

Shared links are read-only and can be revoked by the owner. Access logging may include open count and last-opened time.

Medical disclaimer

Medication Tracker supports documentation and discussion with your prescriber. It does not provide diagnosis or treatment instructions.

This tool supports tracking and clinician discussion. It does not replace professional medical advice.
