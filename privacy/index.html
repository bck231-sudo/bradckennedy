Privacy policy

How AdhdAgenda handles private information

AdhdAgenda stores practical treatment information, allows controlled sharing, and is not a public health profile or medical advice service.

Owner-controlled sharing Read-only recipient access Clinical use support only

Data storage

Medication and wellbeing entries are stored for continuity and consult review. Storage can be local-only or synced depending on tracker settings.

Sharing

Shared links are read-only and can be revoked by the owner. Access logging may include open count and last-opened time.

What this privacy policy is focused on

Owner control

The owner controls what is recorded, when it is shared, and which categories of detail stay visible or hidden in read-only views.

Read-only review access

Sharing is intended for review, not editing. Recipients do not use shared links to change medication records, check-ins, or notes.

Medical disclaimer

AdhdAgenda supports documentation and discussion with a prescriber. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or give treatment instructions.

Questions about visibility?

Use the tracker's sharing controls or contact the owner directly

Privacy settings and read-only link options are designed to keep owner controls separate from recipient review access.
