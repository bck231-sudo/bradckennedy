Terms of use

Terms for using the AdhdAgenda website

AdhdAgenda is intended to support private medication tracking and clinician review. It is not an emergency response tool, a public medical record, or a replacement for professional healthcare advice.

Intended use

This website is intended for private medication tracking and support for clinician review, not for diagnosis or medical instruction.

Account responsibility

Users are responsible for keeping access credentials, private data, and shared links secure.

Key terms in plain language

Use it as a support tool

The website is meant to help organise treatment information, not to replace a prescriber, therapist, GP, or emergency service.

Protect your access

If you use accounts or shared links, you are responsible for keeping them secure and for reviewing whether recipient access is still appropriate.

No emergency service

This website is not monitored as an emergency system. For urgent concerns, contact local emergency services or your care provider directly.
