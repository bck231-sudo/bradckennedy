Terms of use

Usage terms for the Medication Tracker web app.

Intended use

This app is intended for personal tracking and clinician review support.

Account responsibility

Users are responsible for keeping access credentials and shared links secure.

No emergency service

The app is not an emergency response tool. For urgent concerns, contact local emergency services or your care provider.

This tool supports tracking and clinician discussion. It does not replace professional medical advice.
