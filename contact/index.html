Contact and support

Use the right contact path for support, privacy, and clinical matters

Questions about AdhdAgenda, access issues, or website problems belong here. Medication decisions, urgent concerns, and private treatment questions should stay with the right clinical channel.

Website support

For product support, access issues, or workspace problems, email support@adhdagenda.com.

Clinical communication

Medication decisions, treatment changes, and interpretation of symptoms should be discussed directly with your clinician or prescribing clinician.

Urgent concerns

Do not use this website for emergencies. Contact local emergency services or urgent medical support for immediate health concerns.

Privacy and access

If a shared link or access arrangement needs to be changed, revoked, or checked, raise it through the tracker owner rather than through a clinical appointment.

When to use this page

  • Website issues, access problems, and update requests.
  • Questions about what the tracker is for and how it is used.
  • Read-only link issues, privacy visibility questions, or sharing concerns.
Important:

AdhdAgenda supports organisation and discussion. It does not diagnose, prescribe, or replace professional care.
