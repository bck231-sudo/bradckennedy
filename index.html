Common questions

A few sensible questions before you start.

The homepage should answer the practical questions first: setup, sharing, everyday use, and whether the product is meant to replace medical advice.

Do I need to set everything up at once?

No. You can start with your current medication plan and add notes, check-ins, and consult questions over time. It is meant to become more useful as the record grows.

Can I track medication changes and side effects together?

Yes. Changes are meant to sit alongside the reason for the change, the effects you noticed, and anything you want to ask about next.

What can a clinician see if I share a review view?

The shared side is read only and focused on the key review information. It is separate from your main editing workspace.

Is this only useful right before appointments?

No. The daily side is designed for the in-between days as well, so the next review is built from an ongoing record instead of last-minute recall.

Is AdhdAgenda medical advice?

No. It is a private tracking and review tool. It helps organise information and questions, but it does not replace medical advice from a clinician or psychiatrist.
