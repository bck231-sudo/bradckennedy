About the product

Built for two different moments: private daily use and clearer review

AdhdAgenda is designed to keep everyday tracking practical, then surface the useful parts in a calmer format when a clinician or psychiatrist needs to review what has changed.

Owner-first daily use

Quick logging, lightweight notes, and next actions stay easy to keep up with on an ordinary day.

Clinician-friendly review

Current medications, recent changes, side effects, and questions are grouped into a simpler review flow.

Read-only sharing options

Sharing is controlled, limited, and separated from owner editing so the review context stays cleaner.

Why it exists

Reduce friction without losing the detail that matters later

Daily tracking is easier to keep using when it feels lightweight. Appointment preparation still needs structure. The product is built to support both without pushing everything into one crowded screen.

Who it helps

  • People tracking medications, timing, and effects for their own continuity between visits.
  • Appointments where a cleaner summary helps a psychiatrist or clinician scan the key points faster.
  • Situations where read-only access is useful without exposing owner controls or sensitive editing screens.
