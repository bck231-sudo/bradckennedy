About the product

A private daily tracker with a separate review layer when appointments matter

AdhdAgenda is designed for people who need practical day-to-day continuity and a clearer way to prepare for clinician conversations without copying the same notes into multiple places.

Everyday use comes first

Day-to-day tracking stays focused on the owner, not on a report format.

Review is a separate job

The review layer is built to make changes, questions, and patterns easier to scan quickly.

Sharing stays controlled

Read-only access is optional, limited, and kept separate from owner editing tools.

Why it exists

Keep daily tracking simple, then make review readable when the appointment is close

The product exists to reduce friction. It keeps practical details together so the owner does not have to rebuild the story from memory each time treatment is reviewed.

Instead of forcing the daily workspace to look like a report, it separates the review job into a calmer format that is easier for another person to scan.

What stays different

  • The daily area is for the owner to capture what happened without extra noise.
  • The review area groups medications, changes, side effects, and questions into a cleaner appointment-ready structure.
  • Read-only access can be shared when helpful without exposing editing controls or sensitive owner-only workflow.

Where it helps most

Three situations the product is designed to support well

This page focuses on how the product is meant to be used, rather than repeating the home page marketing summary.

Between appointments

Keep medications, timing, check-ins, and quick observations together so continuity is easier to maintain.

Before a review

Bring current medications, recent changes, side effects, and prepared questions into one simpler review flow.

When sharing helps

Provide limited read-only access to a clinician or trusted support person without turning the whole workspace into a shared editing surface.
